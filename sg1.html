SG1 Blood Gas & Electrolyte Analyzer

The Seamaty SG1 is an easy-to-use handheld portable blood gas analyzer that providing accurate blood gas, electrolyte and critical care biochemistry results in just a few minutes, and allowing you to do the diagnosis anywhere and anytime.

Features


Accurate


Compact


Reliable

Key Features & Benefits

- Integrated Analysis: Measures blood gases, electrolytes, and hematocrit.
- Fast Testing: Results delivered in 4 minutes.
- Low Sample Volume: Requires 100µL of whole blood.
- Automatic Operation: Fully automatic calibration, cleaning and maintenance
- Built-in-Printer: Onboard thermal printing.
- User-Friendly: Touchscreen interface for intuitive operation.

Technical Specifications

Measured Parameters: PH, PCO2, Na+, K+, Cl-, Ca2+, Hct, Glu, Lac
Calculated Parameters: TCO2, cHCO3, BE(b), AG, cHGB, BE(ecf), cSO2
Sample Volume: 100µL
Sample Types: Arterial or Venous whole blood
Test Time: Four Minute
Calibration: Fully automatic
Electrode Type: Ion- & gas electrodes
Printer: Built-in thermal printer
Interfaces: USB, Type C
Battery: 3.7V, 5,000 mAh
Display: 4.3 Inch IPS touchscreen
Weight: 600g
