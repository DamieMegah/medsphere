About Us

MedSphere Healthcare Limited is a trusted supplier of medical equipment, laboratory equipment, diagnostic devices, hospital instruments, and medical consumables in Nigeria. We are dedicated to supporting hospitals, laboratories, clinics, healthcare professionals, and medical facilities with reliable, high-quality healthcare solutions.

We offer a comprehensive range of healthcare equipment, including diagnostic equipment, laboratory analyzers, patient monitoring systems, surgical instruments, hospital furniture, and medical consumables. Our extensive product portfolio is designed to meet the evolving needs of modern healthcare institutions.

At MedSphere Healthcare Limited, we are driven by excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is to be a leading healthcare equipment provider in Nigeria, delivering reliable medical solutions that improve patient outcomes and support the advancement of healthcare services across the country.

Our Mission

Medsphere Healthcare Ltd is on a mission to enhance the quality of healthcare in Africa by providing unsurpassing medical devices and healthcare solutions. We strive to be the lifeline that connects medical innovation to patients care.

Our Vision

We envision a future where we are the leading choice in healthcare and our affiliated segments by setting a new standard in quality customer satisfaction.

Values

Quality, Innovation, Reliability, Integrity, Professionalism and Excellence. Serves as the cornerstone values of this enterprise..

Our Services

Consultation

Medsphere Healthcare Ltd prioritizes customer satisfaction and delivers high-quality, patient-centered services. Our consultation process is driven by expert medical knowledge, modern technology, and genuine care — ensuring every client receives tailored advice, prompt attention, and trusted healthcare solutions.

Installation & Set-up

Medsphere Healthcare Ltd, we specialize in the professional installation of a wide range of medical equipment. Our team of certified biomedical engineers ensures that every device is installed with precision and maintained to meet global safety and performance standards.

Maintenance

At Medsphere Healthcare Ltd, we understand that medical equipment reliability is non-negotiable. Our maintenance services are designed to prevent breakdowns, extend equipment lifespan, and ensure optimal performance across all healthcare environments.
