CBS-50 |Electrolyte Analyzer

The CBS-50 delivers rapid and precise electrolyte analysis with minimal maintenance. Designed for high stability and immediate result display, it features intelligent systems that ensure smooth, efficient operation in any clinical setting.

Key Features & Benefits

- Automatic Calibration: Ensures consistent accuracy.
- Fast Result Delivery: Results in approximately 25 seconds.
- Flexible Sample: Supports blood, serum, plasma, or urine(diluted).
- Real-Time display: Immediate result viewing
- High Stability: Long-life electrodes and auto cleaning design.
- Smart System Alerts: Maintenance and reagents monitoring

Technical Specifications

Parameters: K+, Na+, Cl-, Ca2+, pH, TCO2
MethodS: Mult-Biosensor Technology + Pressure Sensor Technology
Throughput: Approx. 80 samples/hour
Sample Types: Whole blood, serum, and plasma
Sample Volume: 60-120µL
Measuring Time: 25 seconds
Calibration: Automatic
Dimension: 390 × 320 × 150 mm
Weight: 8.5kg
Interface: RS-232
Printer: Internal thermal printer
Display: Real-Time results display
Power: AC 80- 260V, 50/60Hz 80W
