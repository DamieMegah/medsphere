SMT-70 | Semi-Auto Chemistry Analyzer

The SMT-70 is a smart biochemistry analyzer device, with incubator that provides a basic solution for clinical diagnosis. and budget friendly solution for clinic chemistry.

Key Features & Benefits

- Photometric Measurement: Utilizes 8-wavelength interference filters (340-630nm) with high accuracy.
- Principle: Colorimetry and Turbidimetry.
- User-Friendly Operation: Large 7-inch color touchscreen with intuitive software.
- Compact & Portable: Lightweight, space-saving design.
- Flexible Analysis Modes: Support kinetic, endpoint, fixed-time, and absorbance modes.
- Built-in Printer: Instant thermal printing of result.
- Reliable: Incubator with selectable 25°C/30°C/37° setting.

Technical Specifications

Wavelength: 340, 405, 492, 510, 546, 578, 620nm
Photometric Range: 0-3.0 Abs
Light Source: Halogen lamp, 6V/10W
Cuvette: Flow cell
Printer: Built-in thermal printer
Dimension: 320 × 254 × 137 mm
Power Supply: DC 24V 6A
Display: 7-Inch color touchscreen
Weight: 3.0kg
